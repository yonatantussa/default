About Me

Hello, I'm Yonatan, a passionate Software Engineer dedicated to continuous learning and innovation in the expansive realm of computer science. My journey began in the 7th grade when I built my first computer, sparking a curiosity that evolved into a never-ending exploration of technology.


Initially drawn to becoming a Hardware Engineer, I broadened my skills in high school by founding and managing a company focused on selling shoes. This entrepreneurial venture provided valuable insights into leadership, marketing, and business expansion. As the company diversified into various markets, I seized the opportunity to merge my technical expertise with customer demand, transforming parts lists into fully functional custom-built computers.


While my initial focus was on hardware, I felt a desire to explore other facets of the computer field. This led me to delve into web/mobile app development and software engineering. I was instantly drawn to the seemingly endless possibilities in systems and software. Throughout my academic journey, I have immersed myself in various programming languages, while dabbling in other aspects of computer science to explore my interests and expand my expertise.


My ultimate goal is to leverage my acquired knowledge and skills to build something of my own, and make a lasting impact within the tech industry.


Explore my portfolio and resume to get a closer look at my skills and experiences. Whether you have inquiries or just want to connect, feel free to reach out.
